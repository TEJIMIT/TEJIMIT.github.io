NEW YORK, NY -- The Vera Institute of Justice (Vera) along with the Andrew W. Mellon Foundation today announced a $250,000 grant to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) to establish a consortium that will support expanding access to postsecondary education to people currently and formerly in prison statewide. Additional consortium members will include the Massachusetts Department of Correction, the Massachusetts Parole Board, Massachusetts Probation Service, the Office of Community Corrections, the Petey Greene Program, and other organizations in the state focused on serving currently and formerly incarcerated individuals.
